About Wisconsin Tech Solutions

Wisconsin Tech Solutions is dedicated to delivering innovative and high-quality software solutions to enhance the operations of businesses across various industries. Our services cover a broad range of areas from maintaining integration infrastructure to creating modern scalable systems.

We specialize in:

  • Maintaining integration infrastructure across multiple platforms ensuring seamless data flow and operations.
  • Refactoring legacy systems into modern cloud-based microservices using Azure and serverless technologies for better scalability and maintainability.
  • Automating deployment pipelines to streamline the deployment process using GitHub Actions, ensuring more efficient and error-free deployments.
  • Enhancing the frontend user experience for platforms such as Shopify by building custom features using Liquid, CSS, HTML, and JavaScript.
  • Providing advanced observability for order processing to ensure accuracy and reduce ticket resolution times for thousands of monthly orders.
  • Developing and maintaining detailed technical documentation and training internal teams for better collaboration and growth.
  • Creating robust Salesforce solutions by transforming complex automation workflows into efficient and maintainable code using Apex.
  • Enhancing Power BI reports to include critical financial data, contributing to more accurate business insights.
  • Conducting rigorous testing to ensure the reliability and performance of all integrations and deployments.

At Wisconsin Tech Solutions, we prioritize reliability, scalability, and innovation in every project we take on. Our team is committed to providing cutting-edge solutions that drive success for businesses.
